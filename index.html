The Future of Education

According to Bernard, by the year 2035, the role of teachers will change significantly. They will no longer be just content transmitters but will become guides and mediators of the learning process.

Education will no longer focus solely on acquiring specific knowledge. The focus will shift to continuous skills development, which should be adapted and improved throughout one's life.

With the support of technology, learning will become increasingly personalized, flexible, and student-centered, preparing individuals for a constantly changing world.
